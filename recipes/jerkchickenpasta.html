Jerk Chicken Pasta





    Ingredients!

  • 4 skinless boneless chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsps. jerk seasoning
  • 2 tbsps. creole seasoning
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 2 tbsps. olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tbsp. minced garlic
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup parmesan
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella
  • 2.5 cup bowtie pasta
  • 1 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 small onion sliced
  • 1/2 small bell peppers sliced
